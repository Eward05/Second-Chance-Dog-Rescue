WHAT WE STAND FOR AT SECOND CHANCE DOG RESCUE

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect, advocate, educate, and offer adoption platforms in order to help build our communities' awareness of dogs' innate needs and to end companion animal homelessness.

Our Vision

We are committed to creating a world where every companion animal is free from enduring human induced pain an suffering through a life of abandonment, abuse and neglect.

Our Values

Compassion, commitment, knowledge, open-mindedness, responsibility, love for animals, sympathy
