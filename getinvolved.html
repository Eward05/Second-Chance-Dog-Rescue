JOIN OUR TEAM

Have a fire in your heart for rescue and want to get involved? Second Chance Dog Rescue is a 100% volunteer run, not-for-profit dog rescue. We've got opportunities for everyone, from casual positions to more core positions. Check em' out below!

VOLUNTEER

Join Us! We're almost entirely volunteer run so without you, and other dog loving, good hearted people like yourself, we'd be scrolling social feeds looking at cute dogs like the rest of the world. Instead, we get to post the pictures and connect amazing dogs with furever family's. Volunteers make the world go round. Speaking of going around the world, you can totally do that too! Dogs. Travel. Good deeds! It's the ultimate package!

DONATE

While being a not-for-profit may sound like a trendy thing to do, it's actually a ton of hard work and hustle. Rescuing dogs is one thing, but fundraising and facilitating clinics is a whole other ball game. Donations big and small allow us to do all the amazing work we get to do. Your dollars literally translate into happy dogs. Donate dollars 4 doggos!

FLIGHT BUDDY

Flight volunteers are essential to Second Chance Dog Rescue, and enable us to bring dogs in from all over the globe. As a flight volunteer, you act as an escort for a rescue dog from one airport to the other. All dog costs are covered by us, and all dogs will be traveling with paperwork and necessary shots.

FOSTER

Here's a lil' tidbit that you might not know, we don't have an actual office. We were working remote before Covid-19 so when bringing dogs back, we need to know we have foster homes ready to give each and every one a home, or else they can't come. No fosters = no dogs. Even if you're not good at math, it's a no brainer. So, foster already!

ADOPT

This might seem obvious but if you want to help us with our mission, being a furever home for a furry friend does just that!

SPONSER

Your gift could get a rescue pup started with their new life. Help us cover customs fees, daily boarding costs or ensure that our new arrival pups get any vet services or vaccinations they need to live their best lives!
