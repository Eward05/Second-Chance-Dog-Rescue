ANY AMOUNT HELPS

Second Chance Dog Rescue does not receive any financial support for this important work from federal, or local government agencies. We rely solely on donations from members of our compassionate, animal-loving community.

Please consider making a one-time donation or become a monthly sponsor. Thank You!

If you are donating on someone's behalf, you can request a donation certificate by emailing: emma@secondchancedogrescue.ca

Please note: we do not issue tax receipts for donations under $20


HOW YOUR DONATION HELPS

Intakes

With your help, we can bring in strays found on the streets, those rescued from cruelty and requiring extra care and rehabilitation, and surrendered pups whose owners can no longer care for them due to unforeseen barriers.

Vetting

Rescue is expensive, not to mention the costs of vetting! Your donation goes directly towards vet exams, treatment, and any required medication due to urgent medical needs, untreated and/or neglected conditions

Training

Training begins the moment we meet those oh so sweet pups. When it comes to donations, you're directly supporting these dogs in putting their best paw forward to set them up for success.



DONATE SUPPLIES

Running a rescue organization isn't cheap. We are so grateful for any donations made, whether it be cash, dog food, toys, collars and leashes, or donating your time to the cause.

The following supplies are always needed:

•  Dog food and treats

•  Martingale Collars

•  Leashes (retractable not accepted)

•  Bowls

•  Toys (new or clean/gently used)

•  Pee pads

•  Cleaning supplies

•  Clean blankets and towels

•  Shampoo & Conditioner (pet only)

•  Crates

•  Medications (example: flea/tic, pain meds, etc) - please email vet@secondchancedogrescue.ca for more details.

•   Please email supplies@secondchancedogrescue.ca if you have items you'd like to donate.
